Steps for Finding your Westwood Parts

There are over 30 types of Westwood tractor built over a 30 year period. We have put together a simple 4 step process to help you locate your model number and year of manufacture to enable you to find your spare parts.

1. Identification Sticker

Find the sticker on your tractor which contains all of the information you need.

2. Model Number and Year of Manufacture

Use the sticker to identify and understand the required tractor numbers.

3. PDF Parts Lists

Download the parts list for your tractor to identify your part.

4. Search for Parts

Input the part number into the search to find your part.

Step 1: Finding your Identification Sticker

The identification sticker is the key to finding all of the information you need about your tractor. Stickers will differ depending on the year of manufacture. Below are images showing the different sticker possibilities:

A. Where is the identification sticker?

Common places to find your sticker are:
  • Under the Seat - lift the seat and look directly underneath
  • Under the Bonnet - lift the bonnet and look at the back of the steering bulkhead
  • Rear of the Tractor - on the left hand side of the chassis above the cutting deck will be the barcode
The image below shows some further possible locations of your identification sticker:

B. What do I do next?

We recommend taking a photo of your sticker as this will be used to identify your machine.
